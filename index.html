About Me

I am a small-town girl who grew up in Alaminos, Laguna in a middle-income family. My father is a retired assistant manager at a bank, and my mother was an agriculturist-turned-OFW. Through hard work and a bit of luck, I now run my own IT company of 60 people, serving clients locally and abroad for software development.

Starting the Digital Transformation of Philippine Banks (2013-2018)

From 2013 to 2018, I played a pivotal role in the digital transformation of Philippine banks. This involved modernizing banking systems, implementing new technologies, and improving the digital experience for customers nationwide.

Startup Weekend San Francisco 2018

In 2018, I won the Startup Weekend San Francisco, beating contestants from around the world in downtown SF. The event was sponsored by Levi's Global, marking a significant milestone in my professional journey.

Founding Umpisa (2019)

In 2019, I co-founded Umpisa with my cousin. We initially collaborated with entrepreneurs and startups at Stanford and Harvard, which paved the way for our big break working with NTUC Singapore in 2021.

Fun Fact

Despite my busy schedule, I always find time for my hobbies, which include hiking, painting, and exploring new technologies. Balancing work and play keeps me motivated and forward-looking!
